## Content Standards

- **Minimum word count:** 1,500 words (target 2,000–2,500)
- **Primary keyword density:** 1–2%
- **Readability target:** 8th–10th grade (Flesch-Kincaid)
- **Internal links per post:** 3–5
- **External references per post:** 2–3

## On-Page SEO Checklist

- [ ] Primary keyword in H1
- [ ] Primary keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Meta title: 50–60 characters, includes primary keyword
- [ ] Meta description: 150–160 characters, includes primary keyword, has CTA
- [ ] Images have descriptive alt text
- [ ] URL slug is short and keyword-rich
- [ ] H2s and H3s use semantic variations of primary keyword
- [ ] Internal links to relevant content
- [ ] External links to authoritative sources (open in new tab)

## Content Quality Standards

All published content must score 70+ on these dimensions:
- **Voice consistency** — matches brand voice and audience level
- **Specificity** — includes concrete examples, data, or mini-stories
- **Structure** — logical flow, clear headers, scannable
- **SEO compliance** — meets on-page checklist above
- **Readability** — passes readability target
