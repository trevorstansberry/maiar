|---|---|

| Email | 150–300 words |
| Social post (LinkedIn) | 150–300 words |

| Ad headline | Under 30 characters |
| Ad description | Under 90 characters |
| Meta title | 50–60 characters |
| Meta description | 150–160 characters |

## Formatting Rules

### Blog Posts
- Use H2 for main sections, H3 for subsections
- Include a TL;DR or key takeaways section
- Break up text with subheads every 300 words max

### Emails
- Subject line: under 50 characters
- Preview text: under 100 characters
- One primary CTA per email

### Social
- Lead with the hook — first line must stop the scroll
- Use line breaks for readability
- Hashtag policy: [none / 3-5 relevant / category]
